Meet Wendy Mupaku

Clinical Social Worker & Psychotherapist

Your Partner in Creating Hope & Growth

With 14+ years of specialised expertise, Wendy provides high-level clinical care in diverse environments, including disability support in higher education and autism early intervention.

She is an established author and researcher on the development of children with ASD and intellectual disabilities, with four peer-reviewed articles and a book chapter to her name. View her publications below.
